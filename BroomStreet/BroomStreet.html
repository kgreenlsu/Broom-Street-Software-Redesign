Services We Provide

We specialize in QAD and Progress® programming, database administration and system integration. In addition Broom Street Software offers turnkey server installations and upgrades for Windows, Linux, and most Unix operating systems, as well as a full range of services to meet your IT business needs. Whether you need to expand the capabilities of your current IT systems or network, upgrade or fine-tune, integrate or Web enable, Broom Street Software has the talent, dedication, and experience to get the job done.


ERP Services

We effectively assist our clients with their challenges relating to enterprise planning, system installation, training, documentation and support. We deliver the results you need in many areas including resource planning, inventories, and shipping.


IT Services

Broom Street can reduce your system downtime and IT overhead with our outsourcing services. Whether you are looking for periodic assistance with system administration or full service support, we can customize a solution that will meet your needs and your budget.


Web Services

As the business world grows more interconnected and more business services are provided over the Internet, the quality and responsiveness of Web applications will help an enterprise maintain or increase market share.

Stephen Mc Hugh CPIM

Stephen has been working with the QAD® system and the Progress® 4GL language and database since December of 1989. As an employee at QAD Inc. starting in 1989, Stephen gained an intimate knowledge of the software enterprise solution QAD. Martin Cohen

Martin has been working in the Software Development and Integrated Circuit Engineering industry since 1990. Currently he serves as Partner and CIO of Broom Street Software that focuses on implementing, customizing, maintaining and training of the QAD ERP system. Martin also served as the IT Manager for an international Integrated Circuit Engineering company for five years that is currently a worldwide leader in the design, development and marketing of silicon timing devices for a number of high-growth application segments. Other experience includes Quality Assurance and Configuration Management positions in the Software Engineering arena.

Mark J. Mosley, MCSE RHCE

Mark has over 10 years of experience working with enterprise networks and server systems. He holds certifications from Microsoft, Red Hat Linux, and Cisco Systems. Since 1998, he has worked in various engineering roles for organizations such as American Express, GE Capital, and Computer Sciences Corporation. Beginning in 2001, Mark served as a full time consultant for the Department of Defense where he worked on a large scale implementation of a VPN network for over 80 military sites.

Dan Schoenfeld

He has expertise on multiple development platforms including iOS, Web, Windows and Cloud-based services.
